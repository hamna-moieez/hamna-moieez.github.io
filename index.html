Slum Mapping and Localization using Remote Sensing Imagery
Undergraduate Thesis, 2020
code / video

In this work, I worked on understanding how slums form and change right inside major metropolitan cities. In addition to collecting dataset from satellite imagery, which included manual markings of slum settlements, I developed a pipeline to monitor, segment and localize slums in several areas of the cities. Such an automated pipeline is important to inform revelant authorities so as to redirect their rehabilitation efforts and resources appropriately.
